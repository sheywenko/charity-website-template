About Us

Smile With Layo Foundation is an NGO involved in health, food and sanitation services for underprivileged. This is a non-profit organization that believes in restoring hope and courage in the underprivileged by advancing the minds of those who are capable of lending a helping hand. SWLF Foundation strongly believes in promoting charitable giving and humanitarian work among the youth by selflessly supporting the act of giving back. We transcend cultural and religious boundaries to create a network of individuals that look to this organization as a project of their own; where no matter your race or creed, you are united with a group of people that shares similar desire to help. SWLF is a non- religious, non-political organization.

To improve the lives of underprivileged in local communities through education, health, economic empowerment and social amenities.

Our vision is to strengthen our global reach and expand our commitment to new communities, focusing on making the world a better place for everyone.

  • To bring smiles to the faces of the people.
  • To bring hope to the underprivileged.
  • To provide underprivileged children with a well-rounded education.
  • To raise a generation of change agents/nation builders.
  • To provide valuable and life changing mentorship to underprivileged children.
  • To raise funds through donations and Sponsorship for the underprivileged.
  • To provide a medium for youths to work together to solve societal issues concerning children.
  • To create awareness and provide aid on health issues associated with children in rural public schools.
